KUALA LUMPUR \u2014 Malaysian social media star Siew Pui Yi is now venturing into the education sector.<\/p>\n

Through an Instagram post on Feb 14, Ms Siew announced that she is offering US$1 million (S$1.3 million) worth of scholarships to underprivileged students.<\/p>\n

According to Ms Siew, also known as ms_puiyi on Instagram, this is one of her ways to give back to society as she understands that not everyone is handed equal opportunities when it comes to education.<\/p>\n

She revealed that she initially wanted to study medicine but could not pursue her dream of becoming a surgeon. This was due to various factors such as financial constraints as she came from an underprivileged family.<\/p>\n

She also shared that she ended up pursuing a degree in business while having to work part-time as a model.<\/p>\n

\u201cThis has always been something that I wish to hopefully make a difference some day, to give equal opportunities to deserving students that can\u2019t afford the education they need to succeed.<\/p>\n

\u201cAnd now, I am pleased to announce that I will be launching my very own scholarship to help students achieve their dreams,\u201d said the 24-year-old.<\/p>\n

Through her partnership with Educapital Foundation, scholarships will be offered to underprivileged students from all over the world including Malaysia.<\/p>\n

\u201cEach student will be granted US$1,000 to pursue whichever educational field that they desire.<\/p>\n

\u201cThey will also be provided access to financial hardship grants while they\u2019re in school as well as business startup grants after graduation,\u201d she added.<\/p>\n

The application process involves applicants revealing the field of study being pursued such as artist management, medical assistant and even cannabis culinary.<\/p>\n

The site also states that the scholarship is valid for only 30 days.<\/p>\n

Previously, Ms Siew made headlines after announcing that she was shutting down her OnlyFans page to focus on her career in music as a DJ.<\/p>\n
